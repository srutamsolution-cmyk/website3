About Us

Strategic Partner for Digital Learning

SRUTAM Solution bridges the gap between complex information and effective knowledge transfer. We are a team of instructional designers and technologists dedicated to delivering measurable training outcomes.

From onboarding and regulatory compliance to workforce upskilling, we provide the technical expertise and strategic direction required to deliver results. We build learning ecosystems designed to scale with your business infrastructure.

Capabilities

Corporate Training & Learning Technologies

End-to-end eLearning development tailored to industry-specific requirements.

Custom eLearning Development

We develop interactive, scenario-based modules designed to engage learners. Our custom courseware is engineered to meet specific performance objectives.

LMS Integration & Support

Comprehensive support for Learning Management System migration and setup. We ensure seamless integration, user management, and reporting configuration.

Video-Based Learning

Professional explainer videos, software simulations, and live-action training. We manage the entire production lifecycle to simplify complex concepts.

Localization & Translation

Effective training for global workforces. We translate and adapt courseware into over 30 languages, ensuring cultural consistency across regions.

Compliance & SCORM Training

Engaging, trackable compliance training that meets legal standards. We build secure, SCORM-compliant courses designed for retention.

Mobile Learning & Microlearning

Responsive learning modules designed for the modern workforce. We deliver accessible training solutions optimized for all devices.
